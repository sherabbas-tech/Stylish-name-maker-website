## ✨ Key Features

-   **🎮 20+ Style Categories** – From *Gun Design* and *Anime* to *Cute*, *Emoji*, *Free Fire*, and *Sad* styles. There's a design for every mood.
-   **📋 One-Click Copy** – Click any style card or the "Copy" button to instantly copy the styled text to your clipboard.
-   **🕒 Recent History** – Automatically saves your last 3 copied styles for quick reuse.
-   **🖼️ Grid / List View** – Toggle between layouts. Your preference is saved locally.
-   **📱 Fully Responsive** – Works seamlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
-   **🚫 Zero Dependencies** – Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No heavy frameworks or external libraries (except Google Fonts for styling).
-   **🔒 Private & Secure** – Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is ever sent to a server.
